Symbiosis

Project Details

-  Mission: Create a game surrounding a global crisis or issue. Project surrounds the bleaching of the coral reef, and our impacts on that environment.

-  Summary: You are a zooxanthellae, an algae responsible for gathering the energy the coral uses to sustain itself and grow. Your life is now a struggle against man-made threats afflicting your home, gather light for energy and keep your home, and yourself, alive.

-  Length: 4 weeks

-  Team Size: 4

-  Platform/Language: Javascript base

-  Contribution: Coral reef reactive art, a particle system that reacts to player progression or struggle to grow or decay the environment the player exists in. Uses basic numeric values representing Life to modify the structure in its entirety.
