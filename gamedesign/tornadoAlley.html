Tornado Alley

Project Details

-  Mission: Create an endless runner

-  Summary: You play as a tornado, travelling across a thin ridge of a mountain range. Get as far as you can while avoiding clouds that represent opposing weather systems that would destroy your form.

-  Length: 2 weeks

-  Team Size: 1

-  Platform/Language: Javascript base

-  Features of note: Tornado is a bezier curve controlled particle system with a fluid control scheme, the groundwork is also defined using the same technique with 4 iterations (foreground: 2 sides, distance: 2 sides).
